Masterclass with the Masters is a piano festival created by Deniza Dërdovski, pianist/entrepreneur. The festival aims to provide a creative and inspiring space for classical pianists in Kosovo, where they will take not only piano lessons with world renown pianists but also be part of mind-triggering lectures about Mindset of an Artist, Managing Performance Anxiety, Memorization, Piano pedagogy, Sight-reading followed by concerts.

THE MOTIVATION BEHIND THE IDEA


The title "Masterclass with the Masters" represents a personal meaning to the project director prof. Dërdovski, as she invites her most influential "piano masters" from around the world to lecture and perform in Kosovo as a form of giving back to her home country and its extraordinary talented artists. This festival will not only be focused on training pianists physically how to play, perform and teach better but also dive into the mental aspect of how to think as an artist and go beyond the classical and academic identity.

Festival will be open for all musicians, artists, music enthusiasts.
Active participants: Pianists
Passive participants/audience: All musicians and artists

Our Goal


The project goal is to inspire students not only by demonstrating how to be a great pianist but also providing lectures, recitals and resources to help students navigate finding their path in their careers after graduation.

There is so much more in an artist than just being sufficient in their field. It takes a vast mental preparation and self-awareness to be a successful, and most importantly, a happy artist. Masterclass with the Masters will provide a non-traditional piano education that will cover all necessary topics and ideas that students might not get from their educational institution.
