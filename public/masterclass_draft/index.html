About

Masterclass with the Masters is a piano festival created by Deniza Dërdovski, pianist/entrepreneur. The festival aims to provide a creative and inspiring space for classical pianists in Kosovo, where they will take not only piano lessons with world renowned pianists but also be part of mind-triggering lectures about Mindset of an Artist, Managing Performance Anxiety, Memorization, Piano pedagogy, Sight-reading followed by concerts.

The title "Masterclass with the Masters" represents a personal meaning to the project director prof. Dërdovski, as she invites her most influential "piano masters" from around the world to lecture and perform in Kosovo as a form of giving back to her home country and its extraordinary talented artists. This festival will not only be focused on training pianists physically how to play, perform and teach better but also dive into the mental aspect of how to think as an artist and go beyond the classical and academic identity.

Festival will be open for all musicians, artists, music enthusiasts. Active participants: Pianists Passive participants/audience: All musicians and artists
